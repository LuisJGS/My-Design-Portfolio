Movie Theater App/Website Project

Name: MoviePlex

Project Overview

Design a movie theater app that unify all movie theater brands under one app to help users save time when looking for nearby locations.

The Problem

The Goal

The users want to have the option to finding different movie theater brands, buy tickets and have the ability to skip the concession stand lines by buying snacks from the app.

Help users to save time, avoid waiting lines and find the best theater option around them, based on location, busy times and the ability to check out from the app / website, including the ability to buy snacks.

User Research: Summary

By interviewing different users from different backgrounds, I found out that users want a reliable, fast and easy way to find a close by theater to enjoy a movie. Some users have busy lives and want to be able to save time and skip the lines by buying tickets and snacks through the app or website. Others want to be able to see what the less busy hours are so they can go with their kids without disturbing the experience for others.

User Research: Pain Points

#1

Finding close by theaters no matter brand.

#2

Checking out through the app or website to save time.

#3

Skip concession stand lines, by buying snacks from app.

#4

Find the less busy time to be able to enjoy movies with family and kids.
